Hi, I'm Carmen!

I'm a graduate student at NYU's Integrated Design & Media program, in my second-to-last semester as we speak. So far, I've...

  • pitched app and web design ideas to a citizen science company partnered with NASA
  • created and coded an Eye that follows you around the room, using Arduino and Processing
  • designed an Easter egg for the interactive mural project, working with the Designing for Creative Physical Computing VIP team

These days I daylight as a library associate in South Florida, running STEM programming for kids of all ages and doing plenty of readers advisory for parents looking to get their kid addicted to reading. In my previous lives, I've been a telescope operator, a learning assistant for the physics lab classes, an art museum gallery guide, a data analyst, and a cash advance underwriter.

Fun Facts:

  • I'm from the Windy City! Then we went south for the winter in 2005 and I lived in Miami for over a decade, finishing my B.A. in Physics at Florida International University.
