Eclipse Soundscapes

Eclipse Soundscapes is a project launched by ARISA Lab. They approached NYU Tandon in Spring 2021 to work with students in User Research Experience to create a new mobile and website layout for their citizen science project.

ARISA Lab specifically wanted to ensure that Eclipse Soundscapes would be accessible to people with low-vision / blindness. The project focuses on recording and analyzing natural sounds that occur during a solar eclipse, and would be used by anyone, scientist or science enthusiast alike.

I worked with Lilly Lin and Kendra Barber, and together we built a prototype with Figma.
