Interactive Mural

The Designining for Creative Physical Computing team is building an interactive mural using Arduino. The mural includes a rushing river bisecting an urban landscape and a natural one, with lit-up buildings on one side and lively bees and trees on the other.

Since joining the team in Fall 2021, I designed rock-shaped potentiometers that would trigger movement from bees affixed to the mural. The rocks would also determine the color of the lights in the buildings, play some music, and when twisted in the right configuration, dispense some seeds-filled confetti as a fun Easter egg.

All our code can be found on the team GitHub, along with instructions on how to make the potentiometers, buildings, and seed dispenser.
