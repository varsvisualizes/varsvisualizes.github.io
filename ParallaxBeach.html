Parallax Beach

Parallax Beach is a project for a class in Motion Graphics.

Parallax Beach was made using Adobe Illustrator and Adobe After Effects. The sunset and beach were drawn in Illustrator, then the assets were imported into After Effects.

This was the original iteration of Parallax Beach, before the waves and clouds took their proper form.
