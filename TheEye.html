The Eye

The Eye was created as a final project for Interactive Objects & Systems, a course in physical computing.

Processing and Arduino came together to create an eye that followed you as you walked around in front of it. Ultrasonic sensors would determine your position, and alarms would sound if you violated The Eye's personal space.

A photocell would tell The Eye if it was dark enough to awaken.

If you leave The Eye to its own devices, it will fall asleep. Activating the sound detector will wake it from its dreams.
