SmartFilter

SmartFilter was created as a final project for User Experience Design. It was inspired by sensory sensitivity towards particular sounds. This lead to research on misophonia and sensory disorders, and the goal to create a product that would help a person feel relief when they might otherwise be triggered by a sound like chewing or clicking.
